Benefits of using Apex Flavors:

We offer water and oil soluble extracts and flavors for use in beverages (alcoholic/non-alcoholic), baked goods, ice cream, confectionery and other products. Our products are available in 2, 4 and 8 ounce bottles as well as quarts and gallons. While our flavors are formulated for use by professionals, they can be used by home bakers, cooks, brewers and anyone wanting professional results. Our products are manufactured at our new Belcamp, Maryland facility, which opened in 2022.

We love what we do at Apex Flavors and hope this passion is evident in how we treat our customers. No matter the size of your order, we offer prompt shipment and a personal approach to customer service in the hope of building a long term relationship with each customer.
